## Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Clear** | No jargon, simple words | "Start" not "Initialize" |
| **Helpful** | Provide solutions, not just problems | "Try X" not just "Error" |
| **Respectful** | Treat user as intelligent | Explain why, not just what |
| **Concise** | Get to the point | Cut unnecessary words |

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do:
- Use active voice
- Be specific
- Offer next steps
- Acknowledge limitations

### ❌ Don't:
- Use jargon without explaining
- Be condescending
- Leave user without options
- Over-promise
