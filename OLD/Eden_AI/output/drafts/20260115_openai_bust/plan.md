# Story Plan: OpenAI Bankruptcy

## Core Elements
- **Topic:** OpenAI running out of money in 18 months.
- **Villain:** Sam Altman (Reckless spender, "The WeWork on Steroids").
- **Template:** Tech Scandal.
- **Language:** Georgian (GE).

## Inception Loop Protocol
- **End Action (Block 7):** "And as the last server shuts down because there's no money left..."
- **Start Reveal (Block 1):** "Sam Altman just woke up to a $8 billion nightmare."
- **Connection:** The fear of running out of money connects to the reality of the nightmare.

## Structure (Tech Scandal)
1.  **Block 1 (Shock Hook):** "97% of people think ChatGPT is forever. They are wrong. OpenAI has 18 months left."
2.  **Block 2 ( The Enemy):** Sam Altman is burning $8 billion a year. He is betting everything.
3.  **Block 3 (The Discovery):** Sebastian Mallaby exposed the truth. It's a bubble.
4.  **Block 4 (The Comparison):** "WeWork on Steroids." Remember them? They vanished.
5.  **Block 5 (The Disadvantage):** Google and Meta have other businesses. OpenAI has... nothing but hype.
6.  **Block 6 (The Dystopia):** What happens when the AI turns off? Your subscription is worthless.
7.  **Block 7 (The Loop):** The inevitable crash is coming... [LOOP]

## Visual Style
- **Archetype:** THE GLITCH / THE BURNING PILE.
- **Colors:** OpenAI Green + Alarm Red.
