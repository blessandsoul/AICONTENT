# The Enemy Protocol

## Purpose
Every story needs a clear, named villain for shares.

## Rules
- Block 1 must IDENTIFY the villain by name (person, company, or system)
- The villain must be BLAMED explicitly, not implied
- Use accusatory language: "приказал", "уничтожил", "скрыл"

## Banned
❌ "Система дала сбой..." (passive, no villain)

## Allowed
✅ "Марк Цукерберг ЛИЧНО приказал удалить доказательства..."

## Villain Types
1. **PERSON:** Elon Musk, Trump, Zuckerberg
2. **COMPANY:** Meta, Google, OpenAI
3. **SYSTEM:** The Algorithm, The Elite, The Government

## Scoring
- Named person villain: +3 points
- Named company villain: +2 points
- Abstract system villain: +1 point
- No clear villain: -3 points
