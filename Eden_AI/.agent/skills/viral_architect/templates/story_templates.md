**Best For:** AI, hacking, data breaches, social media
**Villain Type:** Tech CEO or Company
**Visual Archetype:** THE GLITCH, THE NETWORK
**Emotional Arc:** Shock ‚Üí Betrayal ‚Üí Fear ‚Üí Confusion ‚Üí Personal ‚Üí Dystopia ‚Üí Curiosity

**Hook Pattern:** 
"[COMPANY] just got caught [SHOCKING ACTION] and [STATISTIC]% of users have no idea..."

**Block Structure:**
1. The Discovery (what they found)
2. The Cover-Up (how company hid it)
3. The Retaliation (what they did to whistleblower)
4. The Global Response (bans, investigations)
5. The Stats (how many affected)
6. The Dystopia (we can't escape tech)
7. The Loop (next victim chosen)

## üëë POLITICAL DRAMA

**Best For:** Politicians, elections, government secrets
**Villain Type:** Politician or Government
**Visual Archetype:** THE THRONE, THE ARCHIVE
**Emotional Arc:** Shock ‚Üí Anger ‚Üí Fear ‚Üí Pause ‚Üí Personal ‚Üí Despair ‚Üí Cliffhanger

**Hook Pattern:**
"[POLITICIAN] was just exposed for [SHOCKING ACTION] and the documents prove..."

**Block Structure:**
1. The Leak (what document shows)
2. The Betrayal (who they betrayed)
3. The Money Trail (where money went)
4. The Victims (who suffered)
5. The Cover-Up (how they hid it)
6. The System (why nothing changes)
7. The Loop (next scandal brewing)

## üíÄ TRUE CRIME

**Best For:** Murder, scams, cults, disappearances
**Villain Type:** Criminal or Enabler
**Visual Archetype:** THE ABYSS, THE DECAY
**Emotional Arc:** Shock ‚Üí Horror ‚Üí Mechanism ‚Üí Pattern Break ‚Üí Scale ‚Üí Inevitability ‚Üí Mystery

**Hook Pattern:**
"[VICTIM] was found [SHOCKING STATE] and the last person to see them was..."

**Block Structure:**
1. The Discovery (body/evidence found)
2. The Timeline (last known moments)
3. The Suspect (who had motive)
4. The Investigation (what police found)
5. The Pattern (other victims?)
6. The Twist (unexpected connection)
7. The Loop (case still open)
