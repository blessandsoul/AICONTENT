> Unique environments for Miniature Diorama visuals.

## 1. LABORATORY SETTINGS
- **Clean Room:** White sterile surfaces, airlocks, hazmat suits (1:87), stainless steel.
- **Particle Collider Tunnel:** Curved massive machinery, pipes, warning lights, scale figures looking small.
- **Bio-Dome:** Glass enclosure, alien plants or dangerous virus containment, controlled lighting.
- **Quantum Computer Core:** Gold wiring, super-cooling pipes, absolute zero atmosphere.

## 2. FIELD EXPEDITIONS
- **Arctic Base:** Snow, ice core drilling rig, orange tents, blizzard effect (tilt-shift).
- **Deep Sea Trench:** Submersibles, bioluminescence, pressure hull, dark blue water.
- **Volcano Rim:** Lava flows (miniature), ash clouds, heat shielding equipment.
- **Mars Colony:** Red dust, habitat domes, rover tracks, starry black sky.

## 3. HISTORICAL/ARCHIVE
- **Museum Storage:** Wooden crates, dinosaur bones, artifact shelves, dust motes.
- **Ancient Library:** Infinite scrolls, candle light, secret knowledge atmosphere.
- **Archaeological Dig:** Grid lines, brushes, layers of dirt revealing history.

## 4. ABSTRACT DATA
- **Neural Network City:** Buildings made of glowing neurons, data highways, blue/cyan palette.
- **DNA Helix Forest:** Giant DNA strands as trees, genetic editing tools.
- **Microchip Metropolis:** CPU architecture as a city, gold pins as skyscrapers.

*Each post gets a unique diorama setting!*
